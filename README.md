# Creator Skills Top 10 (TikTok / YouTube / Instagram)

A practical skill bundle for creator teams and AI agents.

- **For humans:** ship better content faster, reduce monetization risk, and improve sponsor outcomes.
- **For agents:** trigger-ready skills with clear task boundaries and reusable workflows.

## Why this bundle

Most creator teams don’t need another generic “content ideas” tool. They need an operating system from:

1. finding real audience demand,
2. writing stronger hooks,
3. turning briefs into deliverables,
4. protecting monetization,
5. tracking what actually drives revenue.

This repo packages exactly that.

## Included skills (10)

1. **creator-search-intent-radar**  
   Find high-intent topics and build a weekly content backlog.

2. **short-video-hook-lab**  
   Generate and rank hook variants for better first-3-second retention.

3. **creator-deal-ops**  
   Run sponsorship ops (scope, revisions, payment tracking).

4. **cross-platform-recut-planner**  
   Recut one idea for TikTok/Reels/Shorts without copy-paste output.

5. **creator-monetization-risk-checker**  
   Pre-publish risk checks to reduce demonetization/distribution risk.

6. **ugc-brief-to-script-engine**  
   Convert brand briefs into creator-native UGC script packs.

7. **creator-attribution-lite**  
   Connect content outputs to business outcomes with lightweight attribution.

8. **batch-content-sprint-os**  
   Plan weekly content sprints with realistic anti-burnout guardrails.

9. **creator-proof-portfolio-builder**  
   Build one-page proof packs for brand outreach.

10. **comment-to-content-recycler**  
   Turn comments/DMs into prioritized, script-ready content ideas.
