## 4) Production Pack

### TikTok Script (native)
**Hook (0–3s):** “If your TikTok Shop views are fine but sales are dead, this is probably why.”

**Body (3–34s):**
1. “Mistake one: you start with product features. People buy outcomes, not specs.”
2. “Mistake two: your first 2 seconds are slow. Lead with the pain they already feel.”
3. “Mistake three: no proof. Show a real before/after, review, or live result.”

**Fix line:** “Use this order: Hook the pain → show proof → give one clear offer.”

**CTA (34–38s):** “Comment **AUDIT** and I’ll send a plug-and-play script format.”

### Reels Script (native)
**Hook (0–4s):** “Your product isn’t the problem—your script flow is.”

**Body (4–36s):**
- “First issue: feature dumping. Replace that with one customer pain sentence.”
- “Second: no emotional opener. Start with ‘If you’re struggling with ___, watch this.’”
- “Third: no trust trigger. Add one proof moment: testimonial, demo result, or UGC clip.”

**Wrap (36–42s):** “Better script structure = better conversion consistency.”

**CTA:** “Save and share this with a creator who needs higher TikTok Shop CVR.”

### Shorts Script (native)
**Hook (0–3s):** “3 TikTok Shop script mistakes that reduce conversion rate.”

**Body (3–35s):**
- “#1 Feature-first opening. Fix: open with buyer pain.”
- “#2 Weak first line. Fix: one sharp promise in the first sentence.”
- “#3 No conversion proof. Fix: add evidence before the CTA.”

**Framework recap:** “Pain → Promise → Proof → Offer.”

**CTA (35–40s):** “Test this structure on your next product Short and track the lift.”
