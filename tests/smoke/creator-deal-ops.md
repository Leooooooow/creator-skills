## Creator Deal Ops — Smoke Test

### 1) Deal scorecard

- **Decision:** **NEGOTIATE** (do **not** accept as-is)
- **Overall risk:** High

| Dimension | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Deliverables | ⚠️ | 3 videos in 5 days is possible but tight; little buffer |
| Revisions | ❌ | Unlimited revisions = major scope-creep risk |
| Usage rights | ❌ | Unclear rights (organic/paid, platforms, duration, region) |
| Payment terms | ⚠️ | Net45 hurts cash flow; higher collection risk |
| Margin confidence | ❌ | No clear guardrails on effort vs fee due to open-ended revisions/rights |

### 2) Recommendation and negotiation terms

**Recommended action:** **Negotiate** with clear redlines.

Required terms before acceptance:
1. **Revision cap:** Max **2 rounds per video** (extra rounds billed).
2. **Usage rights defined in writing:** platform(s), paid vs organic, term length, territory, whitelist/spark usage.
3. **Timeline protection:** milestone approvals within fixed windows; rush fee if turnaround stays at 5 days.
4. **Payment terms:** preferred **50% upfront + 50% on final delivery (Net15)**; fallback **Net30 max**.

If brand rejects these terms, **decline**.

### 3) Delivery checklist (proposed)

- Day 0: Final brief + rights + contract signed
- Day 1: Concepts/scripts submitted
- Day 2: Brand approvals locked
- Day 3: First cuts (3 videos) delivered
- Day 4: Revision round 1
- Day 5: Final exports + handoff

Acceptance criteria per video: format/spec met, approved script intent, one final approval recorded.
