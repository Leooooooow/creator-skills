- “I get views but no orders. What am I saying wrong?”
- “My script sounds awkward/salesy.”
- “People watch till the end, still no checkout.”
- “Should I do UGC style or direct selling?”
- “When I mention price, they disappear.”
- “Is this just the algorithm, not my script?”

## 4) Top 5 content outputs (hooks + script angle + CTA)

### 1) Topic: **Why “views but no orders” happens (and how to fix it fast)**
**Hooks (3):**
1. “If you get views but zero orders, this one missing line is usually why.”
2. “Stop blaming the algorithm before you check this 7-second script gap.”
3. “People watch, nobody buys? Your CTA timing is probably broken.”

**Short script angle:**
Diagnose 3 conversion leaks in sequence: weak pain specificity → no proof moment → vague CTA. Show before/after line rewrites.

**CTA:**
“Comment **LEAK** and I’ll send the 3-point conversion script checklist.”

---

### 2) Topic: **The no-awkward 20-second script template**
**Hooks (3):**
1. “If your script feels awkward, use this exact 20-second structure.”
2. “Don’t ‘sound salesy’: say these lines in this order.”
3. “Confused what to say on camera? Copy this fill-in template.”

**Short script angle:**
Template: Pattern interrupt → specific pain → micro-proof → single action CTA. Include one creator example.

**CTA:**
“DM **TEMPLATE** and I’ll send the plug-and-play script sheet.”

---

### 3) Topic: **Handle price objections before they happen**
**Hooks (3):**
1. “If people disappear after price, your value framing is late.”
2. “Don’t drop price cold—pre-frame it in 2 lines.”
3. “Price objection fix: move this sentence *before* the reveal.”

**Short script angle:**
Teach “value before price”: who it’s for, cost of not solving, then price + risk reversal.

**CTA:**
“Reply with your product + price point; I’ll give a custom 2-line price pre-frame.”

---

### 4) Topic: **Conversion myths killing your scripts**
**Hooks (3):**
1. “Myth: ‘It’s just the algorithm.’ Reality: your script can still convert low reach.”
2. “Copying viral scripts doesn’t guarantee sales—here’s why.”
3. “3 conversion myths keeping creators stuck at zero orders.”

**Short script angle:**
Myth vs reality format with quick proof clips/case snapshots; end with what to test this week.

**CTA:**
“Comment **MYTH** with the belief you had—I’ll reply with a test to run.”

---

### 5) Topic: **UGC story vs direct pitch: which one should you use?**
**Hooks (3):**
1. “UGC or hard sell? Pick based on buyer awareness, not vibes.”
2. “Same product, two scripts—only one converts better for cold traffic.”
3. “Use this simple rule to choose UGC vs direct pitch in 10 seconds.”

**Short script angle:**
A/B comparison: one product, two script styles, expected conversion behavior by audience temperature.

**CTA:**
“Comment **A** (UGC) or **B** (direct), and I’ll send both script versions.”

## 5) Close feedback loop

**Reply first to these comment types (highest value):**
1. “Views but no orders” (urgent + high revenue impact)
2. “Script sounds awkward” (high frequency + easy win)
3. “Price mention kills conversion” (high buyer intent)

**Follow-up question to ask audience:**
