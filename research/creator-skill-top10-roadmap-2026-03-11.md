# Top 10 Skill Roadmap (Creator Needs → Skill Output)

Date: 2026-03-11

## Prioritization logic

- Priority score = Demand urgency × Revenue impact × Build speed
- Audience fit: Growth Creators (5K–50K), creator teams, UGC operators

## Top 10

1. **creator-search-intent-radar**
   - Job: turn platform search signals into weekly topic backlog + script angles
   - Value: reduce “what should I post” uncertainty
   - Status: **initialized**

2. **short-video-hook-lab**
   - Job: generate + evaluate hook variants (first 1–3 sec focus)
   - Value: improve hold rate and early retention
   - Status: **initialized**

3. **creator-deal-ops**
   - Job: brand deal qualification, deliverables, revision scope, payment terms tracking
   - Value: reduce revenue leakage and payment chaos
   - Status: **initialized**

4. **cross-platform-recut-planner**
   - Job: one core idea → TikTok/IG/YouTube-native variants
   - Value: increase output without copy-paste fatigue
   - Status: **initialized + drafted**

5. **creator-monetization-risk-checker**
   - Job: preflight checks for policy/inauthentic/reuse risk before publish
   - Value: reduce demonetization and distribution risk
   - Status: **initialized + drafted**

6. **ugc-brief-to-script-engine**
   - Job: convert brand brief into creator-native UGC scripts
   - Value: speed up paid collab delivery
   - Status: **initialized + drafted**

7. **creator-attribution-lite**
   - Job: map content outputs to business outcomes (clicks, leads, GMV)
   - Value: make performance actionable, not vanity-only
   - Status: **initialized + drafted**

8. **batch-content-sprint-os**
   - Job: weekly batch planning with role/time/asset constraints
   - Value: reduce burnout and missed posting windows
   - Status: **initialized + drafted**

9. **creator-proof-portfolio-builder**
   - Job: generate concise proof decks/case snapshots for brand outreach
   - Value: improve close rate for collaborations
   - Status: **initialized + drafted**

10. **comment-to-content-recycler**
   - Job: mine comments/DM FAQs into short-form content ideas
   - Value: unlock low-cost content loop from existing audience demand
   - Status: **initialized + drafted**
